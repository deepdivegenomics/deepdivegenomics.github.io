Why This Workshop Exists

Most bioinformatics workshops teach you how to run the tools and build the pipeline. But what happens after variant calling? Which variants actually matter? How do you connect them to a clinical diagnosis?

That's the gap we noticed — and that's exactly what this workshop is designed to fill.

📋

What Others Teach

  • Run the tools
  • Build the pipeline
  • Get the VCF file
🧬

What We Teach

  • Understand the algorithms
  • Interpret the variants
  • Connect genomics to real clinical diagnosis

Who Should Attend

Students in bioinformatics, genomics, or computational biology Life science researchers wanting to upskill Clinicians or genetic counselors wanting a computational foundation Anyone who has run a pipeline but never truly understood what it produced
